Vidyut Vega Pvt Ltd is a startup company specializing in electric vehicle manufacturing. It focuses on producing eco-friendly electric vehicles, including electric sight-seeing vehicles and E-Autos. With a commitment to sustainability, Vidyut Vega aims to contribute to greener transportation solutions by developing innovative and energy-efficient products for public and recreational use.

About us

Vidyut Vega Pvt Ltd is a startup company specializing in electric vehicle manufacturing. It focuses on producing eco-friendly electric vehicles, including electric sight-seeing vehicles and E-Autos. With a commitment to sustainability, Vidyut Vega aims to contribute to greener transportation solutions by developing innovative and energy-efficient products for public and recreational use.
